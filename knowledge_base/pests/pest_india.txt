Rice Stem Borer:
- Damage: Deadhearts & white earheads.
- Management: Trichogramma cards, pheromone traps.

Brown Planthopper (Rice):
- Damage: Hopper burn.
- Control: Avoid high nitrogen; apply neem oil.

Fall Armyworm (Maize):
- Damage: Windowing & whorl damage.
- Control: Emamectin benzoate; pheromone traps.

Aphids (General):
- Damage: Leaf curling, sticky honeydew.
- Control: Neem oil, soap sprays.

Whitefly:
- Major virus vector.
- Control: Sticky traps, imidacloprid (label dose).

Fruit Fly (Vegetables):
- Larvae damage fruits.
- Control: Methyl eugenol traps.

Shoot & Fruit Borer (Brinjal):
- Larval boring.
- Control: Pheromone traps.

Red Cotton Bug:
- Red spots on cotton bolls.
- Control: Neem oil.

Cutworms:
- Cut plants at base.
- Control: Soil drenching with chlorpyrifos.

Termites:
- Soil pests.
- Control: Seed treatment with chlorpyrifos.
