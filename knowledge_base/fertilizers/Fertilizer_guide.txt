Nitrogen (N):
- Promotes leaf growth.
- Apply in 2–3 splits.

Phosphorus (P):
- Essential for root growth.
- Apply as basal dose.

Potassium (K):
- Improves drought resistance.

Zinc:
- Corrects stunted growth.
- Use ZnSO₄ @ 25 kg/ha.

Boron:
- Prevents flower drop.
- Use Borax @ 10 kg/ha.

Sulphur:
- Needed for oilseed crops.

Magnesium:
- Corrects interveinal chlorosis.

Calcium:
- Improves fruit quality.

Organic Manure:
- Improves soil structure.

Biofertilizers:
- Rhizobium for pulses
- Azotobacter & PSB for cereals.
