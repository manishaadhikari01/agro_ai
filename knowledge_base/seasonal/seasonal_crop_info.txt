# Seasonal Cropping Patterns in India
# Source: Govt of India Crop Calendar, ICAR

Kharif Season (June–October):
- Major crops: Rice, Maize, Soybean, Cotton, Groundnut, Bajra
- Rain-fed farming dominant

Rabi Season (October–April):
- Wheat, Mustard, Barley, Chickpea, Lentils

Zaid Season (March–June):
- Extra short-duration crops: Watermelon, Cucumber, Moong, Sunflower

Important Notes:
- Crop calendars differ by state; farmers should follow local KVK schedules
