Rice
- Season: Kharif (main); some irrigated rabi in south. Temp 20–35°C. Water: high requirement; prefer puddled soils for transplanted rice.
- Varieties: Swarna, IR64, CR Dhan series (state-specific).
- Source: ICAR Rice Biology & Breeding; Ministry of Agriculture. https://icar.org.in/ https://agricoop.nic.in/

Wheat
- Season: Rabi (Oct–Dec). Temp 12–25°C. Critical irrigation at CRI, flowering, grain fill.
- Varieties: HD-2967, PBW series.
- Source: ICAR-IIWBR. https://icar.org.in/

Maize
- Season: Kharif & Rabi. Well-drained soils; avoid waterlogging.
- Varieties: HQPM, DHM hybrids.
- Source: ICAR-IIMR. https://iimr.icar.gov.in/

Finger Millet (Ragi)
- Drought tolerant; ideal for drylands in southern/central India.
- Uses: food & fodder; calcium-rich grain.
- Source: ICAR & KVK advisories. https://icar.org.in/

Sorghum (Jowar)
- Suited to semi-arid zones; low input.
- Varieties: MSH/CSV hybrids (region-specific).
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Pearl Millet (Bajra)
- Very drought tolerant; Kharif crop in arid zones.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Barley
- Rabi crop in temperate/short-season zones; malting and feed types.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Small millets (Kodo, Little Millet)
- Suitable for marginal soils; high nutrition value.
- Source: ICAR & NFSM resources. https://icar.org.in/
Chickpea (Gram)
- Season: Rabi (Oct–Nov). Requires cool, dry climate during pod fill.
- Varieties: JG, Pusa series.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Pigeon Pea (Arhar/Toor)
- Long-duration crop; important in semi-arid zones.
- Varieties: Pusa Arhar series.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Green Gram (Moong)
- Short-duration; suitable for Zaid & Kharif; promotes cropping intensity.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Black Gram (Urad)
- Good for rice-based cropping systems; responds to phosphorus.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Lentil (Masoor)
- Rabi crop in north; cool temperature required for flowering & pod set.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Horse Gram
- Drought tolerant traditional legume; soil-improving.
- Source: KVK publications. https://kvk.icar.gov.in/
Soybean
- Kharif crop; nitrogen-fixing; wide adoption in central India.
- Varieties: JS series.
- Source: ICAR-IISR. https://icar.org.in/

Groundnut (Peanut)
- Kharif/rainfed; soil: well-drained sandy loam.
- Varieties: TAG, JL series.
- Source: ICAR-CPCRI. https://icar.org.in/

Mustard/Rapeseed
- Rabi crop in north/central India; critical N & S nutrition.
- Varieties: Pusa Bold.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Sesamum (Til)
- Drought-tolerant oilseed for rainfed lands.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Sunflower
- Needs bright sunshine; suitable as rainfed and irrigated.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/
Mango
- Tropical fruit; requires warm season; sensitive to waterlogging.
- Varieties: Alphonso, Banganapalli, Kesar (region-specific).
- Source: ICAR-Horticulture. https://icar.org.in/

Banana
- Requires humid, warm climate; ratooning common.
- Varieties: Robusta, Grand Naine.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Citrus (Orange/Mosambi)
- Grown in subtropical zones; manage irrigation and nutrient balance.
- Source: KVK & ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Guava
- Tolerant to wide soils; sensitive to waterlogging.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Papaya
- Quick yielding; susceptible to ring spot & papaya ringspot virus.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Apple (Himalayan)
- Temperate fruit; requires chill hours; major in north India.
- Source: ICAR-Horticulture. https://icar.org.in/
Tomato
- Warm-season crop; susceptible to late blight, bacterial wilt.
- Varieties: Pusa series.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Potato
- Cool-season tuber crop; pH 5.0–6.0 ideal; manage seed tuber health.
- Varieties: Kufri series.
- Source: ICAR-CPRI. https://icar.org.in/

Onion
- Grown across India; bulb formation sensitive to temperature and day length.
- Varieties: NHRDF types.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Brinjal (Eggplant)
- Vulnerable to shoot & fruit borer; use pheromone traps.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Cabbage & Cauliflower
- Cool-season cole crops; require fertile soil & consistent moisture.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/

Okra (Bhindi)
- Warm-season; short duration; needs regular pickings.
- Source: ICAR. https://icar.org.in/
Cotton
- Grown in central & southern India; Bt hybrids common; critical pest management (bollworm).
- Source: ICAR-CICR. https://cicr.org.in/

Sugarcane
- Long-duration, high-water crop; ratoonable; varieties region-specific.
- Source: ICAR-Sugarcane Breeding Institute. https://icar.org.in/

Tea
- Plantation crop in Assam & south; needs acidic soils & consistent rainfall.
- Source: Tea Board India. https://www.teaboard.gov.in/

Coffee
- Grown in south; shade trees recommended; susceptible to Coffee Berry Borer.
- Source: Coffee Board of India. https://www.indiacoffee.org/

Jute & Kenaf
- Cash fiber crop in eastern India (Bengal & Assam); needs heavy rainfall & alluvial soils.
- Source: ICAR-CIRCOT. https://icar.org.in/
