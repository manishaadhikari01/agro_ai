# Disease Knowledge Base
# Trusted Sources: ICAR, PlantVillage, CIBRC

Tomato – Late Blight:
- Symptoms: Brown lesions, white fungus underside.
- Management: Mancozeb, Metalaxyl; avoid overhead irrigation.

Tomato – Early Blight:
- Concentric ring patterns.
- Management: Chlorothalonil, crop rotation.

Tomato – Leaf Curl Virus:
- Vector: Whitefly.
- Control: Yellow sticky traps, neem oil, imidacloprid (label dose).

Potato – Early Blight:
- Bull’s-eye spots.
- Control: Mancozeb sprays.

Potato – Late Blight:
- Rapid leaf blackening.
- Control: Metalaxyl-M + Mancozeb.

Rice – Blast:
- Diamond-shaped lesions.
- Control: Tricyclazole.

Rice – Bacterial Leaf Blight:
- Yellow edge lesions.
- Control: Copper oxychloride.

Rice – Sheath Blight:
- Moist conditions.
- Control: Validamycin.

Wheat – Rust (Brown/Black/Yellow):
- Orange or black pustules.
- Control: Propiconazole.

Chilli – Powdery Mildew:
- White powder.
- Control: Sulphur dusting.

Chilli – Anthracnose:
- Sunken dark lesions.
- Control: Carbendazim.

Cotton – Boll Rot:
- Fruit rot during rains.
- Control: Carbendazim + Mancozeb spray.

Cotton – Leaf Curl Virus:
- Vector: Whitefly.
- Management: Vector control essential.

Sugarcane – Red Rot:
- Reddish internal tissue.
- Control: Resistant varieties.

Maize – Turcicum Leaf Blight:
- Cigar-shaped lesions.
- Control: Mancozeb.

Maize – Downy Mildew:
- White fungal growth underside.
- Control: Metalaxyl.

Cucumber – Downy Mildew:
- Yellow patches.
- Control: Chlorothalonil.

Brinjal – Fruit & Shoot Borer:
- Larvae bore into shoots.
- Control: Pheromone traps, emamectin benzoate.

Mango – Powdery Mildew:
- White powder on new leaves.
- Control: Wettable Sulphur.

Banana – Panama Wilt:
- Soil fungus
- Control: Use disease-free suckers.

Banana – Bunchy Top Virus:
- Aphid transmitted.
- Control: Roguing + vector control.

Groundnut – Rust:
- Brown pustules.
- Control: Tebuconazole.

Groundnut – Leaf Spot:
- Brown circular spots.
- Control: Mancozeb.

Citrus – Canker:
- Raised corky lesions.
- Control: Copper sprays.

Grapes – Downy Mildew:
- Oil spots.
- Control: Metalaxyl.

Grapes – Powdery Mildew:
- White powder.
- Control: Sulphur dust.

Pomegranate – Bacterial Blight:
- Oily spots.
- Control: Copper oxychloride.
